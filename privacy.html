Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Version: 1.0 pre-release policy

The Unbroken Crown is in pre-release development. This policy will be checked against the final app, enabled services and third-party SDKs before public release.

1. Intended audience

The Unbroken Crown is intended for players aged 13 and above and is not directed to children under 13. Regional store ratings and minimum consent ages may vary.

2. Information the game may process

Depending on the features enabled in the released version, the game may process:

  • Account identifiers required for sign-in and progress restoration.
  • Game progress, settings, achievements and save-state information.
  • Purchase records and entitlement status.
  • Device, crash, performance, integrity and anti-abuse information.
  • Privacy-conscious analytics needed to operate and improve the game.
  • Support communications voluntarily sent by the player.

3. Private profile portrait

A player-selected profile portrait is designed to remain private on the device. The processed portrait is not intended to be uploaded with Safe Progress, advertising data or analytics. A player may need to select the portrait again after reinstalling the game, clearing app data or changing devices.

4. Advertising and consent

The released game may contain optional rewarded advertisements and limited advertisements at appropriate natural breaks. Advertising requests will be controlled by the applicable consent state. The final Data safety declaration will identify the actual advertising and consent SDK behaviour.

5. Purchases

Digital products in a Google Play-distributed build will use Google Play Billing. BMTinventions does not receive the player's complete payment-card details. Purchase tokens, transaction status and entitlement information may be processed to verify, grant, restore or reconcile purchases.

6. Storage and security

BMTinventions intends to use app-specific storage, secure transport, least-privilege access, protected tokens and appropriate retention controls. No method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Sharing

Information may be processed by service providers necessary for features such as Google Play services, billing, saved progress, advertising, consent, integrity, crash reporting or hosting. The final production policy will identify material service categories actually used.

8. Retention and deletion

Information will be retained only for legitimate operational, security, transaction, legal and support purposes. Players may review the Account and Data Deletion page for deletion options.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated when the game, law, platform requirements or service providers change. The effective date and version will be updated.

10. Contact

Privacy questions may be sent to bmtinventions@gmail.com.
