Account and Data Deletion

The game is currently in pre-release development. The final released game will provide an in-app deletion route whenever account creation or account-linked storage is enabled.

Request deletion

To request deletion of an account or account-linked game data, email: bmtinventions@gmail.com

Use the subject:

The Unbroken Crown — Data Deletion Request

Information to include

  • The account email or player identifier connected to the game.
  • A clear statement requesting account deletion, data deletion or both.
  • Enough information to verify ownership without sending passwords or OTPs.

What may be deleted

  • Account-linked profile and progress information.
  • Settings and eligible saved-game information.
  • Support information not required for legal or security purposes.

Information that may be retained

Limited transaction, fraud-prevention, security, dispute, tax or legal records may be retained when required by law, platform obligations or legitimate protection against abuse. Retained information will not be used to continue ordinary gameplay services after account deletion.

Device-local information

Device-local information, including a private portrait, may also be removed by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the game. Uninstalling alone may not delete separately stored account-linked information.

Confirmation

BMTinventions will acknowledge valid deletion requests and provide confirmation after the applicable deletion process is completed, subject to identity verification and legal retention requirements.
