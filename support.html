Player Support

The Unbroken Crown is currently in pre-release development. Public support response targets will be finalised before launch.

Contact support

Email: bmtinventions@gmail.com

Include these details

  • A clear description of the issue.
  • The game version and Android version.
  • The device model.
  • The approximate date and time of the problem.
  • Steps that reproduce the problem.
  • A screenshot only when it does not reveal private information.

Do not send

  • Passwords, OTPs, backup codes or complete payment-card details.
  • Government identity documents.
  • Information belonging to another player.

Purchase support

For purchase issues, include the Google Play order reference when available, but do not send card details, CVV, PIN or OTP. Refund eligibility is governed by applicable law and Google Play policies.
